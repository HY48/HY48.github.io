Who am I?

Hi, I'm Xavier, a 20 year old Undergraduate Student from Minnesota. You probably know me by HY48, Tsana (this one especially if you play Etterna), or TsanaManzana.

I am currently enrolled at the University of Minnesota (CSE) - Twin Cities studying Computer Science with the intention of focusing on Operating Systems.

I have a particular interest in Operating Systems, Systems Programming, and Computer Architecture. Most of what I write is in C, C++, and Rust and I also occasionally program in OCaml (thank you CSCI 2041).

Besides nerd stuff, I enjoy cycling, listening to music (no genre in particular), reading crappy manga, and cooking (it's good for the soul and the wallet).

About this page

I began writing this page with the intention of centralizing my presence online, something I can point towards when people ask who I am, where can they find me, and what do I do. Besides marking my spot online, I also wanted to put some of what I learned in my internet programming course (CSCI 4131) to use.

This also means that in the future I plan to self host this site with subdomains and stuff.
